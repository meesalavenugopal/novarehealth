Project Overview

NovareHealth is a comprehensive telemedicine platform designed for the African healthcare market, enabling patients to consult with verified doctors through video consultations, receive digital prescriptions, and make payments via M-Pesa.

Key Features

Patient Portal

  • Search and discover doctors by specialization, location, and availability
  • Book appointments with preferred time slots
  • Join video consultations directly from the platform
  • View appointment history and upcoming schedules

Doctor Portal

  • Complete profile management with qualifications and experience
  • Set and manage availability schedules
  • Create and send digital prescriptions
  • View patient history and consultation records

Admin Panel

  • Review and verify doctor registrations with KYC documents
  • Manage users
  • View platform analytics and reports
  • Manage specializations and system settings

AI-Powered Features

  • Smart professional bio generation for doctors
  • Intelligent consultation fee suggestions based on experience
  • AI chat assistant for patient queries

Video Consultations

  • Integrated video conferencing for consultations
  • Automatic meeting link generation on booking
  • Meeting reminders and join instructions via email

Email Notifications

  • Professional HTML email templates
  • Doctor registration and approval notifications
  • Appointment confirmation and reminders

M-Pesa Integration

  • Mobile money payment gateway for Africa
  • Secure payment processing and tracking
  • Payment receipts and refund management

Prescription System

  • Digital prescription creation with medicine database
  • PDF prescription generation and download
  • Prescription history for patients and doctors

Health Records

  • Patient electronic health records (EHR)
  • Medical history and consultation notes
  • Secure access to health data
